<R04>
Remarks Prior to Discussions With Prime Minister Andrus Ansip of Estonia 
in Tallinn, Estonia

November 28, 2006

[The Prime Minister's remarks are joined in progress.]

    Prime Minister Ansip. ----once again, thank you very much for your 
support and for the support of your country.
    President Bush. Thank you, Mr. Prime Minister. We're honored to be 
here. It's a great country. And I thank you for your leadership. I'm 
very proud of the economic accomplishments and your contributions to 
peace and liberty around the world. I've really been looking forward to 
coming here. I'm honored that you agreed to--[inaudible]. Thank you.

Note: The President spoke at approximately 9:37 a.m. at the Stenbock 
House. A tape was not available for verification of the content of these 
remarks.
