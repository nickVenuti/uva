<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report by the National Science 
Board

February 14, 2006

To the Congress of the United States:

    Consistent with 42 U.S.C. 1863(j)(1), I transmit herewith a report 
prepared for the Congress and the Administration by the National Science 
Board entitled, ``Science and Engineering Indicators--2006.'' This 
report represents the seventeenth in the series examining key aspects of 
the status of science and engineering in the United States.
                                                George W. Bush
 The White House,
 February 14, 2006.
