Memorandum on Assignment of Functions Relating to the Report to the 
Congress on Implementation of Humanitarian Assistance Code of Conduct

May 12, 2006

Memorandum for the Secretary of State

Subject: Assignment of Functions Relating to the Report to the Congress 
on Implementation of Humanitarian Assistance Code of Conduct

    Pursuant to the authority vested in me by the Constitution and laws 
of the United States, including section 301 of title 3, United States 
Code, the functions of the President under section 2110(c) of the 
Emergency Supplemental Appropriations Act for Defense, the Global War on 
Terror, and Tsunami Relief, 2005 (title II of Public Law 109-13) (22 
U.S.C. 2370b), are assigned to the Secretary of State.
    You are authorized and directed to publish this memorandum in the 
Federal Register.
                                                George W. Bush

Note: This memorandum was released by the Office of the Press Secretary 
on May 15.
