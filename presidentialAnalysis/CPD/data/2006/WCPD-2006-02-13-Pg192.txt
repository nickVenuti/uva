Memorandum on Assignment of Certain Reporting Function

February 3, 2006

Memorandum for the Secretary of the Labor

Subject: Assignment of Certain Reporting Function

    By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and 
laws of the United States, including section 301 of title 3, United 
States Code, I hereby assign to you the function of the President 
contained within section 401(c) of the National Emergencies Act (50 
U.S.C. 1641(c)), to provide the specified final report to the Congress 
in relation to Proclamation 7959 of November 3, 2005.
    This function may be further delegated to the Deputy Secretary of 
Labor.
    You are authorized and directed to publish this memorandum in the 
Federal Register.
                                                George W. Bush

[Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 8:45 a.m., February 7, 
2006]

Note: This memorandum was published in the Federal Register on February 
8. This item was not received in time for publication in the appropriate
