<R04>
Remarks Following a Meeting With Proponents of Democracy in Tallinn

November 28, 2006

    It's an amazing country you have here. They've got an e-government 
system that should be the envy of a lot of nations. They've

[[Page 2094]]

got a tax system that is transparent, open, and simple; people file 
their taxes over the Internet.
    You're doing a fine job, Mr. Prime Minister. Proud of you.

Note: The President spoke at 10:31 a.m. at the Stenbock House. In his 
remarks, he referred to Prime Minister Andrus Ansip of Estonia. A tape 
was not available for verification of the content of these remarks.
