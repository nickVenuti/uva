Administration of Barack H. Obama, 2009 Statement on Former Governor Rod Blagojevich of Illinois January 29, 2009 Today ends a painful episode for Illinois. For months, the State had been crippled by a crisis of leadership. Now that cloud has lifted. I wish Governor Quinn the best and pledge my full cooperation as he undertakes his new responsibilities.
