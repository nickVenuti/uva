Administration of Barack H. Obama, 2009 Statement on Central African Republic National Day December 1, 2009 On behalf of the American people, I extend my best wishes to all those who are celebrating the National Day of the Central African Republic. Our Nation stands with the Central African Republic as both our countries work together to promote peace and prosperity.
