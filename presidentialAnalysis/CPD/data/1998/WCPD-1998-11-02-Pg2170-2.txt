Statement on Emergency Funding for Antidrug Activities

October 28, 1998

    I am making an additional $732 million in emergency funding 
available to support antidrug activities and drug interdiction. These 
funds were provided in the Omnibus Consolidated and Emergency 
Supplemental Appropriations Act, 1999, which I signed into law on 
Wednesday, October 21st.
    Of the total amount I am making available, funds are distributed as 
follows:
