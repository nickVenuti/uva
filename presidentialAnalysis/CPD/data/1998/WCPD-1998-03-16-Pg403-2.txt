<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on Alaska's Mineral 
Resources

March 10, 1998

To the Congress of the United States:

    I transmit herewith the 1996 Annual Report on Alaska's Mineral 
Resources, as required by section 1011 of the Alaska National

[[Page 404]]

Interest Lands Conservation Act (Public Law 96-487; 16 U.S.C. 3151). 
This report contains pertinent public information relating to minerals 
in Alaska gathered by the U.S. Geological Survey, the U.S. Bureau of 
Mines, and other Federal agencies.
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
March 10, 1998.
