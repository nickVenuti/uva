Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on the Helsinki 
Joint Statement on Nuclear Forces Reduction

April 23, 1998

Dear __________:

    As required by section 1229 of the National Defense Authorization 
Act for Fiscal Year 1998 (Public Law 105-85), attached is a report on 
the Helsinki Joint Statement.
    Sincerely,
                                            William J. Clinton

Note: Identical letters were sent to Strom Thurmond, chairman, and Carl

National Security.
