Administration of Barack H. Obama, 2010 Statement on the Resignation of Dennis F. Hightower as Deputy Secretary of Commerce July 15, 2010 I thank Dennis for his service to the country and to my administration. I appreciate his guidance and hard work on various important priorities, from expanding broadband Internet access across the country to reforming our export control system in a way that enhances our competitiveness and our security. I wish him the best in his future endeavors. Categories: Resignations and Retirements : Commerce, Department of, Deputy Secretary, resignation, statement. Names: Hightower, Dennis F.
