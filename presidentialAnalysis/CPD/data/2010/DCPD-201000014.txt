Administration of Barack H. Obama, 2010 Statement on Senator Harry Reid January 9, 2010 Harry Reid called me today and apologized for an unfortunate comment reported today. I accepted Harry's apology without question because I've known him for years, I've seen the passionate leadership he's shown on issues of social justice, and I know what's in his heart. As far as I am concerned, the book is closed. Categories: Statements by the President : Reid, Sen. Harry. Names: Reid, Harry. Subjects: Congress : Senate :: Majority leader; Nevada : U.S. Senator.
