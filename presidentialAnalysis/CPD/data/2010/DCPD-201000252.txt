In Witness Whereof, I have hereunto set my hand this twelfth day of April, in the year of our Lord two thousand ten, and of the Independence of the United States of America the two hundred and thirty-fourth. BARACK OBAMA [Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 11:15 a.m., April 14, 2010] NOTE: This proclamation was published in the Federal Register on April 15.
