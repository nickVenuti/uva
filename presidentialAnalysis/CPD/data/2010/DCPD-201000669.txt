Administration of Barack H. Obama, 2010 Statement on the Death of Former Senator Ted Stevens August 10, 2010 A decorated World War II veteran, Senator Ted Stevens devoted his career to serving the people of Alaska and fighting for our men and women in uniform. Michelle and I extend our condolences to the entire Stevens family and to the families of those who perished alongside Senator Stevens in this terrible accident.
