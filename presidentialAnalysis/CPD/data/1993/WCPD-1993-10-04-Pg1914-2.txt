Nomination for Ambassador to Poland

 September 28, 1993

    The President announced his intention today to nominate Polish-born 
business consultant Nicholas Rey to be the U.S. Ambassador to the 
Republic of Poland.
    ``I am very proud of this choice,'' said the President. ``Nicholas 
Rey has already done much for America in helping Poland along the road 
to democracy and free markets. I am confident that as our Nation's 
Ambassador, he will continue to further those important values.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
