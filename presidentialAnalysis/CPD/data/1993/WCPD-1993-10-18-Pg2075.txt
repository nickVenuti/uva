Nomination for Ambassadors to Morocco, Sweden, and the Bahamas

 October 14, 1993

    The President announced his intention today to nominate Marc Charles 
Ginsburg to be Ambassador to the Kingdom of Morocco, Sidney Williams to 
be Ambassador to the Commonwealth of the Bahamas, and Thomas L. Siebert 
to be Ambassador to Sweden.
    ``It gives me great pleasure to make this announcement today,'' said 
the President. ``Each of these three outstanding individuals has 
contributed in his own way to the well being of his community. Through 
their efforts, they will ensure that our country maintains its strong 
relationships with our allies in Morocco, the Bahamas, and Sweden.''

Note: Biographies of the nominees were made available by the Office of 
the Press Secretary.
