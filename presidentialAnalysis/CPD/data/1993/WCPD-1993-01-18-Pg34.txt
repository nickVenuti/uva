Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting the Report on Federal 
Advisory Committees

 January 11, 1993

Dear Mr. Speaker:  (Dear Mr. President:)

    In accordance with the requirements of section 6(c) of the Federal 
Advisory Committee Act, as amended (Public Law 92-463; 5 U.S.C. App. 2, 
sec. 6(c)), I hereby transmit

[[Page 35]]

the Twenty-first Annual Report on Federal Advisory Committees for fiscal 
year 1992.
    Sincerely,
                                                   George Bush

Note: Identical letters were sent to Thomas S. Foley, Speaker of the 
House of Representatives, and Dan Quayle, President of the Senate.
