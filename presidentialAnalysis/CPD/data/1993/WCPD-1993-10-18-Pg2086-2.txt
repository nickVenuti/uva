Digest of Other White House Announcements

________________________________________________________________________


The following list includes the President's public schedule and other 
items of general interest announced by the Office of the Press Secretary 
and not included elsewhere in this issue.

________________________________________________________________________


October 9

    In the morning, the President and Hillary and Chelsea Clinton 
traveled to New Haven, CT, where they met with a group of Special 
Olympics participants and a group of fourth graders from the Clinton 
Avenue School in Woolsey Hall at Yale University. Later that evening, 
they returned to Washington, DC.

October 12

    In the afternoon, the President traveled to Raleigh-Durham, NC. He 
then went to the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill where he 
attended an opening ceremony commemorating the university's 
bicentennial. After the ceremony, the President attended a reception at 
George Watts Hill Alumni Center and returned to Washington, DC, in the 
late evening.

October 13

    In the evening, the President and Hillary Clinton hosted a Hispanic 
Heritage Month reception.

October 14

    In the morning, the President met with Members of Congress on NAFTA.
    In the afternoon, the President spoke by telephone with Chief 
Warrant Officer Michael Durant in Mogadishu, Somalia.

October 15

    The President announced his intention to nominate George W. Haley to 
be a Commissioner of the Postal Rate Commission. Haley recently 
completed a term as the Commission's Chairman.
