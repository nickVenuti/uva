Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the Federal Labor 
Relations Authority

 October 26, 1993

To the Congress of the United States:

    In accordance with section 701 of the Civil Service Reform Act of 
1978 (Public Law 95-454; 5 U.S.C. 7104(e)), I have the pleasure of 
transmitting to you the Fourteenth Annual Report of the Federal Labor 
Relations Authority for Fiscal Year 1992.
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
October 26, 1993.
