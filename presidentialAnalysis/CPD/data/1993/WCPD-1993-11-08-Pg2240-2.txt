Nomination for an Assistant Secretary of Commerce

 November 1, 1993

    The President announced his intention today to nominate research and 
international trade consultant Sue E. Eckert to be the Assistant 
Secretary of Commerce for Export Administration.
    ``Sue Eckert brings a wide range of both public and private sector 
experience in international trade to this position,'' said the 
President. ``That experience will be invaluable as we seek to expand our 
country's exports to create more jobs here at home.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
