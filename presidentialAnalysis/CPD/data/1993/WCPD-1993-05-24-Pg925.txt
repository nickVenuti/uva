<R05>
Digest of Other White House Annoucements

________________________________________________________________________


The following list includes the President's public schedule and other 
items of general interest announced by the Office of the Press Secretary 
and not included elsewhere in this issue.

________________________________________________________________________


May 15

    In the afternoon, the President and Hillary Clinton attended the 
U.S. Air Force Thunderbirds aerial demonstration at Andrews Air Force 
Base, Camp Springs, MD. Later, they traveled to New York City where they 
remained overnight.

May 16

    In the afternoon, the President and Chelsea Clinton attended a 
family picnic at Sidwell Friends School. Later in the afternoon, the 
President attended a health care meeting.

May 17

    In the morning, the President traveled to Los Alamos, NM, where he 
toured the Supercomputer Center and the Plasma Implantation Facility at 
the Los Alamos National Laboratory. Later that afternoon, the President 
traveled to San Diego, CA, where he remained overnight.

May 18

    In the morning, the President traveled to Los Angeles, CA, where he 
toured the Los Angeles Valley College lab.
    In the afternoon, the President visited a sporting goods store in 
south central Los Angeles where he played a short game of basketball 
with members of the community.
    In the evening, the President returned to Washington, DC.

May 19

    In the morning, the President went to Capitol Hill where he met with 
the members of the Democratic Caucus and Democratic leaders.
    The White House announced that the President has invited Namibian 
President Sam Nujoma to meet in Washington, DC, on June 16.

May 20

    In the afternoon, the President had lunch with the Vice President.

May 21

    In the evening, the President met with President George Vassiliou of 
Cyprus.
