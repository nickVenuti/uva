Nomination for Ambassador to Brunei

 September 17, 1993

    The President today announced his intention to nominate career 
Foreign Service officer Theresa Anne Tull as Ambassador to Brunei.
    ``Theresa Anne Tull has spent her entire life serving our country in 
the Foreign Service,'' the President said. ``I am certain she will use 
that experience to represent America well overseas and am proud that she 
has accepted this challenge.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary. This item was not received in time for publication in 
the appropriate issue.
