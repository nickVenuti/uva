Appointment of Special Assistant to the President and Deputy Press 
Secretary

 December 14, 1993

    The President announced today that he has appointed Ginny Terzano to 
be Special Assistant to the President and Deputy White House Press 
Secretary. The appointment is effective January 1.
    ``The perspective that comes with Ginny's experience in the media 
and as a spokeswoman will make her a strong addition to our 
communications team,'' said the President. ``I look forward to her 
joining us here at the White House.''

Note: A biography of the appointee was made available by the Office of 
the Press Secretary.
