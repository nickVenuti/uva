Nomination of Edward Warner and Charles Freeman To Be Assistant 
Secretaries of Defense

 March 30, 1993

    The President announced his intention today to nominate Edward 
Warner to be Assistant Secretary of Defense for Strategy and Resources 
and Charles Freeman to be Assistant Secretary of Defense for Regional 
Security.
    ``Ted Warner and Charles Freeman are two of the most outstanding 
people working on defense issues today,'' said the President. ``I am 
extremely pleased that they are joining Secretary Aspin at the 
Pentagon.''

Note: Biographies of the nominees were made available by the Office of 
the Press Secretary.
