Letter to Congressional Leaders Reporting on Budget Rescissions

 June 4, 1993

Dear Mr. Speaker:  (Dear Mr. President:)

    In accordance with the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control 
Act of 1974, I herewith report six proposed rescissions, totaling $176.0 
million in budgetary resources.
    These proposed rescissions affect the Departments of Housing and 
Urban Development, Justice, and Transportation. The details of the 
proposed rescissions are contained in the attached reports.
    Sincerely,
                                            William J. Clinton

Note: Identical letters were sent to Thomas S. Foley, Speaker of the 
House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate.
