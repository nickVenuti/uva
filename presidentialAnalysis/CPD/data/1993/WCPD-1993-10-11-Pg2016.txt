<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the National 
Institute of Building Sciences

 October 6, 1993

To the Congress of the United States:

    In accordance with the requirements of section 809 of the Housing 
and Community Development Act of 1974, as amended (12 U.S.C. 1701j-
2(j)), I transmit herewith the

[[Page 2017]]

16th annual report of the National Institute of Building Sciences for 
fiscal year 1992.
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
October 6, 1993.
