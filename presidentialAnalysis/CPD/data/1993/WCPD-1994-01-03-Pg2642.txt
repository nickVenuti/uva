<R05>
Digest of Other White House Announcements

________________________________________________________________________


The following list includes the President's public schedule and other 
items of general interest announced by the Office of the Press Secretary 
and not included elsewhere in this issue.

________________________________________________________________________


December 27

    In the morning, the President went to Cambridge, MD, where he went 
duck hunting at Tieder Farm. He returned to the White House in the 
afternoon.
    In the late afternoon, the President and Hillary and Chelsea Clinton 
traveled to Hot Springs, AR.
    In the evening, Hillary and Chelsea Clinton went to Little Rock, AR, 
and the President remained overnight in Hot Springs, AR.
    The White House announced that the President has declared a major 
disaster exists in the Commonwealth of Virginia and that he has ordered 
Federal aid to supplement State and local recovery efforts in the area 
struck by severe storms and tornadoes on August 6.
    The White House announced the President will make a state visit to 
Moscow on January 12-15, 1994, at the invitation of President Boris 
Yeltsin of Russia.

December 28

    In the morning, the President went to Little Rock, AR. Following a 
shopping trip in the afternoon, the President and Hillary Clinton 
attended a private reception at the Old State House.
    In the evening, the President and Hillary and Chelsea Clinton went 
to Fayetteville, AR, where they attended an Arkansas Razorback 
basketball game at Bud Walton Arena. They remained overnight in 
Fayetteville.

December 29

    In the late morning, the President and Hillary and Chelsea Clinton 
traveled to Hilton Head, SC, where they attended the 1994 Renaissance 
Weekend.
