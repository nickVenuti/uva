Nomination of Mortimer L. Downey To Be Deputy Secretary of 
Transportation

 March 18, 1993

    President Clinton today announced his intention to nominate Mortimer 
L. Downey, the Executive Director and Chief Financial Officer of the 
Metropolitan Transit Authority of New York City, to be the Deputy 
Secretary of Transportation.

    ``There are few people in this country who can match the experience 
or the expertise

[[Page 452]]

of Mortimer Downey,'' said the President. ``I am very pleased that he is 
joining Secretary Pena at a Department that will play a key role in 
implementing my economic plan, as well as in improving our Nation's 
transportation system.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
