Nomination for Ambassador to the Organization for Economic Cooperation 
and Development

 April 26, 1993

    The President announced today that he intends to nominate David 
Aaron to be Ambassador to the Organization for Economic Cooperation and 
Development.
    ``David Aaron is an experienced and accomplished foreign policy 
hand, who has already been of great service to me as an adviser during 
my campaign and an emissary in Europe before I was inaugurated,'' said 
the President. ``I am confident he will serve our country capably at 
OECD.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
