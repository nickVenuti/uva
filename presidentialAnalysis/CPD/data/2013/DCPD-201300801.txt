Administration of Barack Obama, 2013 Statement on the Upcoming State Visit of President François Hollande of France November 22, 2013 Michelle and I look forward to welcoming President Hollande and Valérie Trierweiler on a state visit to the United States, to include a state dinner at the White House on February 11, 2014. The United States and France are close friends and allies, including through NATO, and our countries have worked together to support democracy, liberty, and freedom at home and abroad for more than two centuries. During the visit, we will discuss opportunities to further strengthen the U.S.-France security and economic partnership. NOTE: The statement referred to Valérie Trierweiler, partner of President Hollande. Categories: Statements by the President : France, President François Hollande, upcoming state visit.
