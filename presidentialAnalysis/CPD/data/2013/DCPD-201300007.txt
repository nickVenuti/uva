Administration of Barack Obama, 2013 Memorandum on the National Flood Insurance Program January 7, 2013 Memorandum for the Secretary of Homeland Security Subject: National Flood Insurance Program I have reviewed your request for approval to issue notes to the Secretary of the Treasury in excess of $20.725 billion, but not to exceed $30.425 billion, for the National Flood Insurance Program and am hereby granting approval for you to do so.
