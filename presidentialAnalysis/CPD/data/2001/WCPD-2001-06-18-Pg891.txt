Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report of the National Endowment 
for Democracy

June 11, 2001

To the Congress of the United States:

    In accordance with the provisions of section 504(h) of Public Law 
98-164, as amended (22 U.S.C. 4413(i)), I transmit herewith the Annual 
Report of the National Endowment for Democracy for fiscal year 2000.
                                                George W. Bush
 The White House,
 June 11, 2001.

Note: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on 
June 13.
