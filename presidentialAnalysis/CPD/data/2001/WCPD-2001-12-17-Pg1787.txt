Executive Order 13239--Designation of Afghanistan and the Airspace Above 
as a Combat Zone

December 12, 2001

    Pursuant to the authority vested in me as President by the 
Constitution and the laws of the United States of America, including 
section 112 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (26 U.S.C. 112), I 
designate, for purposes of that section, Afghanistan, including the 
airspace above, as an area in which Armed Forces of the United States 
are and have been engaged in combat.
    For purposes of this order, I designate September 19, 2001, as the 
date of the commencement of combatant activities in such zone.
                                                George W. Bush
 The White House,
 December 12, 2001.

 [Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 11:38 a.m., December 
13, 2001]
