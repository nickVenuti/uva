Joint Statement by President George W. Bush and President of the Russian 
Federation Vladimir V. Putin on Upcoming Consultations on Strategic 
Issues

July 22, 2001

    We agreed that major changes in the world require concrete 
discussions of both offensive and defensive systems. We already have 
some strong and tangible points of agreement. We will shortly begin 
intensive consultations on the interrelated subjects of offensive and 
defensive systems.
