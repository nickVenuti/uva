Memorandum on the National Flood Insurance Program

November 21, 2005

Memorandum for the Secretary of Homeland Security

Subject: National Flood Insurance Program

    I have reviewed your request for approval to issue notes to the 
Secretary of the Treasury in excess of $3.5 billion, but not to exceed 
$18.5 billion, for the National Flood Insurance Program and am hereby 
granting approval for you to do so.
                                                George W. Bush
