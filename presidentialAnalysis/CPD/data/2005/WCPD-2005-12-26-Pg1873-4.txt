<R04>
Statement on the House of Representatives Passage of Immigration Reform 
Legislation

December 16, 2005

    I applaud the House for passing a strong immigration reform bill. 
America is a nation built on the rule of law, and this bill will help us 
protect our borders and crack down on

[[Page 1874]]

illegal entry into the United States. Securing our borders is essential 
to securing the homeland. I urge the Senate to take action on 
immigration reform so that I can sign a good bill into law.
