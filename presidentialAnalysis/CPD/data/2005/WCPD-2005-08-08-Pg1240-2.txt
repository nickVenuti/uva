Statement Congratulating King Abdallah bin Abd al-Aziz Al Saud of Saudi 
Arabia

August 1, 2005

    On behalf of the United States, I congratulate my friend King 
Abdallah bin Abd al-Aziz Al Saud on assuming the Saudi throne and the 
position of Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques. We wish Saudi Arabia 
peace and prosperity under his leadership. I have spoken today to the 
new King, and the United States looks forward to continuing the close 
partnership between our two countries.
