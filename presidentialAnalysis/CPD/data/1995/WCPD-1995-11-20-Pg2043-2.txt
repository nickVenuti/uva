 Digest of Other White House Announcements

The following list includes the President's public schedule and other 
items of general interest announced by the Office of the Press Secretary

November 12

    In the evening, the President and Hillary Clinton attended the 
Ford's Theatre Gala at the theater.

November 13

    The President announced his intention to appoint Gila Joy Bronner to 
the U.S. Holocaust Memorial Council.
    The President announced the appointment of Marvin F. (Bud) Moss to 
the National Historical Publications and Records Commission.
