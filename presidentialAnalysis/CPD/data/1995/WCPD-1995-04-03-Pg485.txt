Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on the Health Care for 
Native Hawaiians Program

March 27, 1995

To the Congress of the United States:

    I transmit herewith the Report on the Health Care for Native 
Hawaiians Program, as required by section 11 of the Native Hawaiians 
Health Care Act of 1988, as amended (Public Law 102-396; 42 U.S.C. 11701 
et seq.).
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
March 27, 1995.
