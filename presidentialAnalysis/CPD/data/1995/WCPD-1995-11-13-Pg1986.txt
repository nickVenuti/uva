<R04>
Statement on the Shooting of Prime Minister Yitzhak Rabin of Israel

November 4, 1995

    I am outraged at the news of this attempt on the life of Yitzhak 
Rabin, a great leader for peace and a good friend. I join all Americans 
in prayers for his recovery.
