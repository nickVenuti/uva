Letter to Congressional Leaders on Haiti

April 10, 1995

Dear Mr. Speaker:  (Dear Mr. President:)

    Attached, pursuant to section 3 of Public Law 103-423, is the sixth 
monthly report on the situation in Haiti.
    Sincerely,
                                            William J. Clinton

Note: Identical letters were sent to Newt Gingrich, Speaker of the House
