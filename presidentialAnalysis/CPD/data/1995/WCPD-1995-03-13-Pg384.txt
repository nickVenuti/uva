<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the Federal Council 
on the Aging

March 8, 1995

To the Congress of the United States:

    In accordance with section 204(f) of the Older Americans Act of 
1965, as amended (42 U.S.C. 3015(f)), I transmit herewith the Annual 
Report for 1994 of the Federal Council on the Aging. The report reflects 
the Council's views in its role of examining programs serving older 
Americans.
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
March 8, 1995.
