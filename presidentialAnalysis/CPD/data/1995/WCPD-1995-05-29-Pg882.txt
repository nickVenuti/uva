<R04>
Statement on the Hospitalization of Former Defense Secretary Les Aspin

May 20, 1995

    I was saddened to hear that former Defense Secretary Les Aspin was 
hospitalized earlier today. Hillary and I wish him a speedy recovery. 
Our prayers are with him and his family at this time.
