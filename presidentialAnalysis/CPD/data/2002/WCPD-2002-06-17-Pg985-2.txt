<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report of the Interagency Arctic 
Research Policy Committee

June 6, 2002

To the Congress of the United States:

    As required by section 108(b) of Public Law 98-373 (15 U.S.C. 
4107(b)), I transmit herewith the Ninth Biennial Report of the 
Interagency Arctic Research Policy Committee (February 1, 2000, to 
January 31, 2002).
                                                George W. Bush
 The White House,
 June 6, 2002.
