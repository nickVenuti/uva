<R04>
Remarks Opening the 2002 Olympic Winter Games in Salt Lake City

February 8, 2002

    On behalf of a proud, determined, and grateful nation, I declare 
open the games of Salt Lake City, celebrating the Olympic winter games.

Note: The President spoke at 9:10 p.m. at Rice-Eccles Olympic Stadium.

issue.
