Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report of the Corporation for 
Public Broadcasting

June 6, 2002

To the Congress of the United States:

    As required by section 19(3) of the Public Telecommunications Act of 
1992 (Law 102-356), I transmit herewith the report of the Corporation 
for Public Broadcasting for calendar year 2001.
                                                George W. Bush
 The White House,
 June 6, 2002.

Note: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on 
June 11. An original was not available for verification of the content 
of this message.
