<R04>
Statement on the Death of
Prime Minister John Compton of Saint Lucia

September 11, 2007

    Laura and I offer our condolences to the people of Saint Lucia on 
the death of Prime Minister John Compton. The father of Saint Lucia's 
independence, Sir John was an elder statesman of the Caribbean. We will 
miss his vision and leadership, and we extend our deepest sympathies to 
his family.
