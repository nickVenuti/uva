Notice--Intention To Enter Into a Free Trade Agreement With Panama

March 30, 2007

    Consistent with section 2105(a)(1)(A) of the Trade Act of 2002, I 
have notified the Congress of my intention to enter into a free trade 
agreement with the Republic of Panama.

    Consistent with section 2105(a)(1)(A) of that Act, this notice shall 
be published in the Federal Register.
                                                George W. Bush
 The White House,
 March 30, 2007.

 [Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 11:26 a.m., March 30, 
2007]

Note: This notice was published in the Federal Register on April 2.
