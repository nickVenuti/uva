<R04>
Statement on the Death of Representative Juanita 
Millender-McDonald

April 22, 2007

    Laura and I are deeply saddened by the death of Congresswoman 
Juanita Millender-McDonald of California. She was a dedicated public 
servant who tirelessly and honorably served her country for many years. 
We hold Rep. Millender-McDonald's family, friends, staff, and 
constituents in our thoughts and prayers.
