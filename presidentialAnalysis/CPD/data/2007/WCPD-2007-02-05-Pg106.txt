Statement on the Death of Molly Ivins

January 31, 2007

    Molly Ivins was a Texas original. She was loved by her readers and 
by her many friends, particularly in central Texas. I respected her 
convictions, her passionate belief in the power of words, and her 
ability to turn a phrase. She fought her illness with that same passion. 
Her quick wit and commitment to her beliefs will be missed. Laura and

[[Page 107]]

I send our condolences to Molly Ivins's family and friends.
