Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the Department of 
Transportation

April 8, 1997

To the Congress of the United States:

    As required by section 308 of Public Law 97-449 (49 U.S.C. 308(a)), 
I transmit herewith the Annual Report of the Department of 
Transportation, which covers fiscal year 1995.
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
April 8, 1997.
