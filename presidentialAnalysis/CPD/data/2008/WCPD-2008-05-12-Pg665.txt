Memorandum on Assignment of Reporting Function Under Subsection 1225(a) 
of the John Warner National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 
2007

May 6, 2008

Memorandum for the Secretary of State

Subject: Assignment of Reporting Function Under Subsection 1225(a) of 
the John Warner National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2007

    By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and 
the laws of the United States of America, including section 301 of title 
3, United States Code, I hereby assign to you the function of the 
President under subsection 1225(a) of the John Warner National Defense 
Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2007 (Public Law 109-364).
    You are authorized and directed to publish this memorandum in the 
Federal Register.
                                                George W. Bush

[Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 8:45 a.m., May 13, 2008]

Note: This memorandum will be published in the Federal Register on May 
14.
