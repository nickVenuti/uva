<R04>
Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Treasury Department 
Report

June 30, 1994

Dear Mr. Chairman:

    As required by section 511 of the Foreign Relations Authorization 
Act, FY 94-95 (Public Law 103-236), I hereby transmit the report 
prepared by the Treasury Department on expenditures from blocked 
accounts.
    Sincerely,
                                            William J. Clinton

Note: Identical letters were sent to Lee H. Hamilton, chairman, House 
Committee on Foreign Affairs, and Claiborne Pell, chairman, Senate 
Committee on Foreign Relations.
