<R04>
Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting the President's Report on 
Immigration

October 18, 1994

Dear Mr. Speaker:  (Dear Mr. President:)

    I transmit herewith the President's Report on Immigration. This 
interim report is required by section 141(i) of the Immigration Act of 
1990, as amended (Public Law 101-649).
    Sincerely,
                                            William J. Clinton

Note: Identical letters were sent to Thomas S. Foley, Speaker of the 
House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate.
