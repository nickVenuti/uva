Nomination for U.S. Attorney for Delaware

April 12, 1994

    The President today nominated Gregory Moneta Sleet as the U.S. 
Attorney for the District of Delaware.
    ``Gregory Moneta Sleet's extensive legal background and experience 
in the State of Delaware make him an excellent choice for this most 
important judicial position,'' the President said.

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
