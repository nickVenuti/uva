Nomination for Controller of the Office of Management and Budget

October 12, 1994

    The President today announced his nomination of G. Edward DeSeve as 
Controller of the Office of Management and Budget (OMB).
    ``Ed DeSeve is a financial innovator with financial management 
expertise in both the

[[Page 2000]]

public and private sectors,'' the President said. ``He is a welcome 
addition to OMB and will help the administration meet its goal of 
strengthening financial management throughout the Federal Government.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
