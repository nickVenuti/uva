Nomination for Ambassador to the United Kingdom

March 22, 1994

    The President today announced his intent to nominate Admiral William 
Crowe, Jr., as Ambassador to the United Kingdom of Great Britain and 
Northern Ireland.
    In announcing this nomination, President Clinton said, ``Admiral 
Crowe has distinguished himself throughout four decades of dedicated 
public service. I am very pleased that he will continue his service to 
this Nation and that I will be able to rely on his wise counsel in this 
very important position.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
