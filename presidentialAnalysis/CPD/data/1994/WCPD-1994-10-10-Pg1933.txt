<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting Reports of the Department of 
Transportation

October 3, 1994

To the Congress of the United States:

    I transmit herewith the 1993 calendar year reports as prepared by 
the Department of Transportation on activities under the Highway Safety 
Act and the National Traffic and Motor Vehicle Safety Act of 1966, as 
amended (23 U.S.C. 401 note and 15 U.S.C. 1408).
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
October 3, 1994.
