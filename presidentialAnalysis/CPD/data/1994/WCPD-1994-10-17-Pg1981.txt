<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting the Railroad Retirement Board 
Report

October 6, 1994

To the Congress of the United States:

    I hereby submit to the Congress the Annual Report of the Railroad 
Retirement Board for Fiscal Year 1993, pursuant to the provisions of 
section 7(b)(6) of the Railroad Retirement Act and section 12(1) of the 
Railroad Unemployment Insurance Act.
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
October 6, 1994.

Note: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on 
October 7. This item was not received in time for publication in the 
appropriate issue.
