Nomination for United States District Court Judges

September 23, 1994

    The President today nominated the following three individuals to 
serve on the U.S. District Court: John D. Snodgrass for the Northern 
District of Alabama, Sven E. Holmes for the Northern District of 
Oklahoma, and Vicki Miles-LaGrange for the Western District of Oklahoma.
    ``These nominees will be outstanding additions to the federal 
bench,'' the President said. ``They bring experience and excellence in 
the legal profession.''

Note: Biographies of the nominees were made available by the Office of 
the Press Secretary.
