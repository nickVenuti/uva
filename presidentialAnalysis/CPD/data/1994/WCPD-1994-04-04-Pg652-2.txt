Nomination for Ambassador to Algeria

March 30, 1994

    The President today announced his intention to nominate Ronald E. 
Neumann, of California, as Ambassador to Algeria.
    ``Ronald Neumann has exhibited dedication and diplomacy throughout 
his career,'' the President said. ``His experience in the Middle East 
makes him uniquely qualified for this important position.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
