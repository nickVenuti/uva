<R04>
Nomination for Director of the Office of Federal Contract Compliance 
Programs at the Department of Labor

January 13, 1994

    The President today announced his intention to nominate Shirley J. 
Wilcher as Director of the Labor Department's Office of Federal Contract 
Compliance Programs (OFCCP).
    ``Throughout her career, Shirley Wilcher has dedicated her efforts 
to preventing discrimination in America's workplace and educational 
institutions,'' the President said. ``I am pleased she has agreed to 
accept this important position.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
