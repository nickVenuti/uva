<R04>
Statement by the President on the Closing of the Embassy of Rwanda

July 15, 1994

    The United States cannot allow representatives of a regime that 
supports genocidal massacre to remain on our soil.

Note: This statement was part of a Press Secretary statement announcing 
the closing of the Embassy of Rwanda and ordering all personnel to leave 
the United States.
