<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on the Prevention of 
Nuclear Proliferation

May 16, 1994

To the Congress of the United States:

    As required under section 601(a) of the Nuclear Non-Proliferation 
Act of 1978 (Public Law 95-242; 22 U.S.C. 3281(a)), I am transmitting a 
report on the activities of United States Government departments and 
agencies relating to the prevention of nuclear proliferation. It covers 
activities between January 1, 1993, and December 31, 1993.
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
May 16, 1994.
