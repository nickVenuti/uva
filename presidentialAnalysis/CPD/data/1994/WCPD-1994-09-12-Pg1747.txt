<R04>
Statement on the Cuba-United States Agreement on Migration

September 9, 1994

    This agreement, when carried out, will help ensure that the massive 
flow of dangerous and illegal migration will be replaced by a safer, 
legal, and more orderly process.

Note: This statement was included in statement by the Press Secretary 
announcing the agreement reached in the New York migration talks.
