Appointment of Vice Chair and Members of the President's Committee on 
Mental Retardation

July 26, 1994

    The President today announced the appointment of Valerie J. Bradley 
to be Vice Chair of the President's Committee on Mental Retardation 
(PCMR) and Jane Browning of Maryland, Michael Remus of Nebraska, 
Elizabeth Pittinger of Pennsylvania, and T.J. Monroe of Tennessee as 
members.
    ``Valerie Bradley's outstanding knowledge and commitment to the 
field promise a revitalization of the committee and innovative programs 
for the future,'' the President said. ``The newly announced team will 
contribute significantly to renew innovative and beneficial programs at 
PCMR.''

Note: Biographies of the appointees were made available by the Office of 
the Press Secretary.
