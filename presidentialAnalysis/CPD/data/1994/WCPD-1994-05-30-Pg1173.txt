<R04>
Statement on the Whale Santuary Agreement

May 27, 1994

    We are pleased that we were able to get so many other countries to 
agree to a sanctuary. The United States will continue to exercise 
leadership in seeking international agreement on the conservation of 
whales.

Note: This statement was part of a statement by the Press Secretary on 
the International Whaling Commission agreement to create a sanctuary for 
whales.
