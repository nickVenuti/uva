<R04>
Statement on the Executive Order on Energy Efficiency and Water 
Conservation at Federal Facilities

March 8, 1994

    For too long, we have paid too much to heat, cool, and light Federal 
buildings. That's why I'm directing all agencies across the Nation to 
make profitable investments in energy efficiency, investments that will 
benefit the

[[Page 478]]

environment and the taxpayer. This initiative makes Government work 
better and cost less.

Note: This statement was part of a White House press release announcing 
the signing of Executive Order 12902.
