Nomination for an Associate Judge on the Superior Court of the District 
of Columbia

April 12, 1994

    The President has nominated Rhonda Reid Winston to serve on the 
Superior Court of the District of Columbia.
    ``Rhonda Winston's solid legal background and dedication to justice 
will be a great asset to the DC Superior Court and the Nation's 
Capital,'' the President said.

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
