<R04>
Statement on the National Award for Museum Services

October 13, 1994

    This national honor is a tribute to the power of museums to engage 
children, families, and communities, in towns and cities all across 
America.

Note: This statement was included in a White House statement announcing 
the National Award for Museum Services.
