Administration of Barack Obama, 2011 Statement on the Earthquake in Turkey October 23, 2011 We have been following reports of the earthquake in Turkey's eastern province of Van with great concern. On behalf of the American people, I express my deepest condolences to the families of the victims. Our thoughts and prayers are with the brave men and women who are working to bring assistance to this stricken region. We stand shoulder to shoulder with our Turkish ally in this difficult time and are ready to assist the Turkish authorities. Categories: Statements by the President : Turkey :: Earthquake.
