Administration of Barack Obama, 2011 Statement on Representative Lynn C. Woolsey's Decision Not To Seek Reelection June 28, 2011 A fierce advocate for children, families, and the people of the North Bay, Lynn Woolsey has never forgotten the people who sent her to Congress for nearly two decades. She is a leader on progressive causes and a fighter for working families, and we will miss her passionate voice in Congress. Michelle and I wish her well and join the people of California in thanking her for her many years of service. Categories: Statements by the President : Woolsey, Rep.
