Administration of Barack Obama, 2011 Message to the Congress on 2010 Census Redistricting January 5, 2011 To the Congress of the United States: Pursuant to title 2, United States Code, section 2a(a), I transmit herewith the statement showing the apportionment population for each State as of April 1, 2010, and the number of Representatives to which each State would be entitled.
