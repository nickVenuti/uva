Administration of Barack Obama, 2011 Statement on the Observance of Ash Wednesday March 9, 2011 Michelle and I join with millions of Christians here and across the world to mark Ash Wednesday. As we observe the season of Lent, we receive with thanksgiving this opportunity for grace and repentance, recommit ourselves to our faith, and remember our obligations to one another.
