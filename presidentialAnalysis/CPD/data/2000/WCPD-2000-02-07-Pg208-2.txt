<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting a Revision of the United States 
Arctic Research Plan

February 1, 2000

To the Congress of the United States:

    Pursuant to the provisions of the Arctic Research and Policy Act of 
1984, as amended (15 U.S.C. 4108(a)), I transmit herewith the sixth 
biennial revision (2000-2004) to the United States Arctic Research Plan.
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
February 1, 2000.
