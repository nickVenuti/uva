<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report of the Department of 
Transportation

October 18, 2000

To the Congress of the United States:

    I transmit herewith the Department of Transportation's Calendar Year 
1998 reports on Activities Under the National Traffic and

[[Page 2511]]

Motor Vehicle Safety Act of 1966, the Highway Safety Act of 1966, and 
the Motor Vehicle Information and Cost Savings Act of 1972, as amended.
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
October 18, 2000.

Note: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on 
October 19.
