<R04>
Remarks in Marlton

October 18, 2004

    Listen, thank you all for coming. So you're wondering what a 
Republican Presidential candidate is doing in New Jersey 15 days until 
the election? I'll tell you, what I'm doing here is I'm sending a strong 
message that with your help, we can carry New Jersey on November the 2d.
    I'm so honored you all came out to say hello. Thank you so very much 
for your friendship, for your support. Make sure your friends go to the 
polls. Make sure your neighbors go to the polls. And tell them if they

[[Page 2453]]

want a safer America, a stronger America, and a better America, to put 
me and Dick Cheney back into office.
    God bless you all. I'm headed down to Florida. I'm working. We're 
going to win. God bless. Thanks for coming.

Note: The President spoke at 2:18 p.m. at the Evesham Recreation Center. 
A tape was not available for verification of the content of these 
remarks.
