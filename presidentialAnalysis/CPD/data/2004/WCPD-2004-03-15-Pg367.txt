<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on Implementation of the 
Debt Reduction Authority

March 8, 2004

To the Congress of the United States:

    Consistent with section 1321 of the Foreign Relations Authorization 
Act, Fiscal Year 2003 (Public Law 107-228), I transmit herewith a report 
prepared by my Administration on implementation of the debt reduction 
authority conferred by title XIII, subtitle B of Public Law 107-228.
                                                George W. Bush
 The White House,
 March 8, 2004.
