<R04>
Remarks to Army Reservists and National Guard Troops Departing for Iraq 
From Bangor

September 23, 2004

    The President. I want to thank you all very much.
    Troops. Hooah!
    The President. I appreciate your service to the country. I 
appreciate being in the presence of such fine men and women. May God 
bless you all. May God keep you safe. May God bless America as well. 
Thank you all.

Note: The President spoke at 5:47 p.m. aboard a World Airlines plane at 
the Air National Guard Base at Bangor International Airport. A tape was 
not available for verification of the content of these remarks.
