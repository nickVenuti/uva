Executive Order 13344--Amending Executive Order 13261 on the Order of 
Succession in the Environmental Protection Agency

July 7, 2004

    By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and 
the laws of the United States of America, including the Federal 
Vacancies Reform Act of 1998, 5 U.S.C. 3345, et seq., it is hereby 
ordered that Executive Order 13261 of March 19, 2002, is amended as 
follows:
    Section 1. In section 2, subsections (a), (b), and (c) are deleted 
and replaced with the following new subsections (a), (b), and (c):
    (a) Assistant Administrator, Office of Solid Waste;
    (b) Assistant Administrator for Toxic Substances;

[[Page 1213]]

    (c) Assistant Administrator (Air and Radiation).
                                                George W. Bush
 The White House,
 July 7, 2004.

 [Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 9:42 a.m., July 8, 
2004]

Note: This Executive order was published in the Federal Register on July
