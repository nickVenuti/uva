<R04>
Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting the ``U.S. Ocean Action 
Plan''

December 17, 2004

Dear Mr. Speaker:  (Dear Mr. President:)

    Consistent with section 4 of the Oceans Act of 2000 (Public Law 106-
256; 33 U.S.C. 857-19), I transmit herewith the ``U.S. Ocean Action 
Plan,'' a report and statement of proposals prepared by the Council on 
Environmental Quality in response to the Commission on Ocean Policy's 
final recommendations.
     Sincerely,
                                                George W. Bush

Note: Identical letters were sent to J. Dennis Hastert, Speaker of the 
House of Representatives, and Richard B. Cheney, President of the 
Senate. This item was not received in time for publication in the 
appropriate issue.
