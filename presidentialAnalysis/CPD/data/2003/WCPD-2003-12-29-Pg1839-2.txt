<R04>
Statement on the Earthquake in Iran

December 26, 2003

    Laura and I heard this morning of the earthquake centered in the 
city of Bam, Iran. We are greatly saddened by the loss of life,

[[Page 1840]]

injuries, and widespread damage to this ancient city. I extend my 
condolences to all those touched by this tragedy. The thoughts of all 
Americans are with the victims and their families at this time, and we 
stand ready to help the people of Iran.
