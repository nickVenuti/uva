<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting the Federal Ocean and Coastal 
Activities Report

March 19, 2003

To the Congress of the United States:

    In accordance with section 5 of the Oceans Act of 2000 (33 U.S.C. 
857-19), I transmit herewith the first biennial Federal Ocean and 
Coastal Activities Report as prepared by my Administration.
                                                George W. Bush
 The White House,
 March 19, 2003.
