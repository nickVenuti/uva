<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on United States 
Participation in the United Nations and Its Affiliated Agencies

March 19, 2003

To the Congress of the United States:

    I am pleased to transmit herewith a report prepared by my 
Administration on the participation of the United States in the United 
Nations and its affiliated agencies during the calendar year 2001. The 
report is required by the United Nations Participation Act (Public Law 
264, 79th Congress).
                                                George W. Bush
 The White House,
 March 19, 2003.
