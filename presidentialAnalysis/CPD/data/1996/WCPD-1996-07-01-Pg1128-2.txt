<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting Budget Deferrals

June 24, 1996

To the Congress of the United States:

    In accordance with the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control 
Act of 1974, I herewith report one revised deferral of budgetary 
resources, totaling $7.4 million.

[[Page 1129]]

The deferral affects the Social Security Administration.
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
June 24, 1996.
