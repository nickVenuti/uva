<R04>
Message to the Congress on Waivers for Environmental Management

April 26, 1996

To the Congress of the United States:

    I hereby report that I have exercised the authority provided to me 
under subsection 325(c) of the Department of the Interior and Related 
Agencies Appropriations Act, 1996, to suspend subsection 325(a) and 
325(b) of such Act. A copy of the suspension is attached.
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
April 26, 1996.

Note: The President exercised authority to waive certain sections of the 
Omnibus Consolidation Rescissions Act of 1996 (P.L. 103-134) pursuant to 
provisions of that act.
