<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the National Science 
Foundation

May 22, 1996

To the Congress of the United States:

    As required by the provisions of section 3(f) of the National 
Science Foundation Act of 1950, as amended (42 U.S.C. 1862(f)), I 
transmit herewith the combined annual reports of the National Science 
Foundation for fiscal years 1994 and 1995.
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
May 22, 1996.
