Message to the Congress Transmitting Budget Deferrals and Rescissions

February 23, 1996

To the Congress of the United States:

    In accordance with the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control 
Act of 1974, I herewith report three new deferrals and one revised 
deferral, totaling $3.6 billion, and four rescission proposals of 
budgetary resources, totaling $140 million.
    These deferrals affect the International Security Assistance 
programs as well as programs of the Agency for International 
Development. The rescission proposals affect the Department of Defense.
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
February 23, 1996.
