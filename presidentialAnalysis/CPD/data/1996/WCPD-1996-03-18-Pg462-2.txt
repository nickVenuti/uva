Memorandum on Assistance to Slovenia and the Former Yugoslav Republic of 
Macedonia

March 8, 1996

Presidential Determination No. 96-18

Memorandum for the Secretary of State

Subject: Eligibility of Slovenia and the Former Yugoslav Republic of 
Macedonia to be Furnished Defense Articles and Services Under the 
Foreign Assistance Act and the Arms Export Control Act

    Pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 503(a) of the 
Foreign Assistance Act of 1961, as amended, and section 3(a)(1) of the 
Arms Export Control Act, I hereby find that the furnishing of defense 
articles and services to the Governments of Slovenia and the Former 
Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia will strengthen the security of the 
United States and promote world peace.
    You are authorized and directed to report this finding to the 
Congress and to publish it in the Federal Register.
                                            William J. Clinton

Note: This memorandum was released by the Office of the Press Secretary 
on March 11.
