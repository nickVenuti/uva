<R04>
Message to the Congress Transmitting the National Money Laundering 
Strategy for 1999

September 23, 1999

To the Congress of the United States:

    As required by the provisions of section 2(a) of Public Law 105-310 
(18 U.S.C. 5341(a)(2) ), I transmit herewith the National Money 
Laundering Strategy for 1999.
                                            William J. Clinton
The White House,
September 23, 1999.
